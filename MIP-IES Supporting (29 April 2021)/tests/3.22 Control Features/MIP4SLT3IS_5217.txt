Test description

Test IDMIP4SLT3IS_5217Test TitleCreate and update Holdings as ControlFeatures.Execution Priority1ObjectiveBSO validation. To verify basic functionality regarding Holdings (not yet clear what a 'Holding' within MIP4 will be, see remarks on MIP4-IES Test Case Directory): test adding and updating Persons, Equipment and Consumable Materiel Holdings.ScenarioTwo nations are exchanging information between their C2ISs. Nation A will add Holding information to an Organisation and send it to Nation B. Nation A will then update the Holding information, and send the updated information to Nation B.EnvironmentInternet or Co-located. Exchange Pattern: R/R, P/S.Participation2MTRSApplicablePre-test ConditionsNation B has a subscription to Nation A's 'Forces' topic. A friend Organisation has been created by Nation A in the 'Forces' topic.Test InputsN/A.ConclusionHolding information created by Nation A is received by Nation B. Updates to existing Holding information made by Nation A are received by Nation B. Holding information is shared correctly by the C2ISs of both nations.Test OutputsN/A.TraceabilityREQ_INF_0002.
